I am currently a Bioinformatics Ph.D. student in [Dr. Vivek Swarup's lab](https://swaruplab.bio.uci.edu/). My thesis project utilizes statistical methods and computation tools to study Alzheimer's disease, which can potentially be used to identify novel therapeutic targets.

I graduated with a Master in Biotechnology from [The Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/) in 2018. I worked with [Dr. Lewis Chodosh](https://www.med.upenn.edu/apps/faculty/index.php/g275/p20564) at the University of Pennsylvania to develop treatments for recurrent breast cancer and applied statistical methods to quantify the drug effects among 4 different preclinical drug trials. At UPenn, I also participated in the [Penn Biotech Group (PBG)](https://pennbiotechgroup.org/), a student-led Healthcare Consulting Club, helping small biotech start-ups to form business plan and do market research.
